Ganesh Ji

Lord Ganesha embodies wisdom, prosperity, new beginnings, and the remover of obstacles.

Hanuman Ji

Lord Hanuman symbolizes immense strength, devotion, courage, wisdom, and unwavering faith.

Radha Krishan

Radha Krishna symbolize eternal love, divine devotion, purity, and spiritual unity.

Durga Mata

Durga Mata embodies strength, courage, protection, and the triumph of good over evil.

Bholenath

Bholenath, the compassionate Lord Shiva, symbolizes purity, power, and divine peace.

Shree Ramchandra

Shree Ramchandra, the embodiment of truth, virtue, courage, and righteousness.

Khatushyam

Khatushyam Ji, the merciful lord of faith, sacrifice, and devotion.

Baba Ramdev

Baba Ramdev Ji, the divine protector and healer of the humble.
